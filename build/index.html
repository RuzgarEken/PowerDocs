Power

A universal stat and effect system for Unity.

User Friendly

Create game mechanics by connecting Power assets. Most gameplay systems can be built without writing custom code.

Stat Agnostic

Power has no predefined stats. Every stat's purpose and behavior are defined entirely by the relationships you create.

Effect Centric

Go beyond primitive values. Power provides specialized stat types that enable rich gameplay interactions and complex effects.

Minds Its Own Business

Power operates within a well-defined scope, allowing you to extend it freely in your own game logic.
